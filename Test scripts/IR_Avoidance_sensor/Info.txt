3 pins
out GND VCC

out = Digital output 
Low = obstacle
high = clear

GND = Ground

VCC = connect to 3V-5V

Emits light from LED and detects reflections from the photodiode
has a build in potentiometer that allows distances from 2cm-30cm
Turning the potentiometer clockwise increases the distance, Counterclockwise decrease the distance

In the Lafvin 2wd the Right is connected on D9 and left on D5

| Left | Right | Meaning        | Action      |
| ---- | ----- | -------------- | ----------- |
| HIGH | HIGH  | Clear          | FORWARD     |
| LOW  | HIGH  | Obstacle LEFT  | TURN RIGHT  |
| HIGH | LOW   | Obstacle RIGHT | TURN LEFT   |
| LOW  | LOW   | Obstacle BOTH  | STOP / BACK |
